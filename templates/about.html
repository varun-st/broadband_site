Company History

TnTel was born out of a vision to become the most admired in-home entertainment, education, and interactive services provider in India, back in 2000.

A Startup Company

We started with the motto of creating a digital culture for India. We are one of the fastest growing startups in the world and own a place in the Top 50 list of Most Innovative Companies of the World. We offer ultra-affordable data services to the people through our best in-class 4G infrastructure. We also offer a wide range of Digital Services. We have our own suite of apps which offers a wide range of services like Live TV, Music Streaming, Online Newspapers and Magazines etc.
